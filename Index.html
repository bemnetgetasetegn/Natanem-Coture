About us

Natanem Couture stands out for creating elegant and exclusive pieces with a constant focus on craftsmanship and individuality

History

Founded in the heart of Addis Ababa, Natanem Couture began as a bold vision to redefine Ethiopian elegance through bespoke hat design. What started as a small workshop crafting custom headpieces for local events soon evolved into a recognized house of haute couture. With deep roots in traditional craftsmanship and a keen eye for global fashion trends, Natanem Couture became the first haute couture hat maker in Ethiopia, blending heritage and innovation in every stitch.
